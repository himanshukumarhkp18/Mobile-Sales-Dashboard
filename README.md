#  Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** for analyzing mobile phone sales performance across different **cities, brands, months, and payment methods**.  
It helps identify **revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance** for better business decision-making.

##  Project Objective
To analyze mobile phone sales data and provide insights on:
- Sales performance across time (month, quarter, year)
- Top-performing brands and models
- Customer purchase behavior
- Payment method preferences
- Regional (city-wise) sales distribution

---

##  Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes:

-  **City-wise sales map**
-  **Monthly & Quarterly filters**
-  **Year-wise sales donut chart**
-  **Model-wise quantity sold**
-  **Payment method analysis (UPI, Debit Card, Cash, Credit Card)**
-  **Customer rating distribution**
-  **Day-wise sales trend**
-  **Average customer age by mobile model**

## Business Insights
- **OnePlus** has the highest quantity sold.
- **UPI and Debit Cards** are the most used payment methods.
- **Weekend sales** are higher than weekdays.
